What YOU Need To Know About Tacos

Let's Taco Bout It

Tacos are a traditional mexican dish. It is made with a tortilla, a thin flat pancake of cormeal or flour,
and it consists of a filling inside. The fillings can vary from beef, pork, vegtables, or anything your heart desires.
There is a gret versatility and variety when talking about tacos.If you're feeling adventurous you could try tacos
made out of cow tongue or one of the exotic meats that are fantastic in tacos.

Click here to learn about exotic meats people put in their tacos.

Tacos are a finger food that can also come with toppings. Toppings do not mean peanuts and M&Ms like on your
frozen yogurt, but salsa and vegtables. Most people prefer things like cilantro, lemon, salsa, onions, ect
You decide how much extra flavor you wouldlike to add to your tacos.
